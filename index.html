🐾WC LineageBETA

Family trees for
Warrior Cats roleplay.

A free, fan-made family-tree editor for the Warriors RP community. Build your trees cat by cat: clans, ranks, moons, mates, mentors, litters, identity, and portraits, on a canvas that keeps the lines clean. Everything runs in your browser from a single file, fully offline. No accounts, no telemetry; your cats stay on your machine.
